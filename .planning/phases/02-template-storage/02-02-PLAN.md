  <action>
    Create 12 template JSON files following the LegalTemplate schema from Plan 01.

    **BAIL APPLICATION templates (3 files):**

    All bail applications need these required_fields:
    - applicant_name (party): Full legal name of accused
    - applicant_address (text): Residential address
    - fir_number (text): FIR/Case number, e.g., "FIR No. 123/2024"
    - police_station (text): Name of police station
    - sections_charged (text): Sections under which accused is charged
    - date_of_arrest (date): Date when accused was arrested
    - grounds_for_bail (text): Legal grounds for seeking bail
    - relief_sought (relief): Specific bail relief requested

    Optional fields:
    - surety_details (text): Details of proposed surety
    - previous_bail_history (text): Any prior bail applications

    Template content placeholder: "IN THE {court_name}\n\nBAIL APPLICATION\n\nIn the matter of:\n{applicant_name} ... Applicant\n\nVersus\n\nState of {state} ... Respondent"

    **LEGAL NOTICE templates (3 files):**

    Required fields:
    - sender_name (party): Name of notice sender
    - sender_address (text): Full postal address of sender
    - recipient_name (party): Name of notice recipient
    - recipient_address (text): Full postal address of recipient
    - subject_matter (text): Brief description of matter
    - demand_details (text): Specific demands/reliefs sought
    - compliance_period (text): Time period for compliance, e.g., "15 days"

    Optional fields:
    - prior_correspondence (text): Reference to earlier communications
    - supporting_documents (text): List of attached documents

    **AFFIDAVIT templates (3 files):**

    Required fields:
    - deponent_name (party): Full name of person making affidavit
    - deponent_address (text): Residential address
    - deponent_occupation (text): Occupation of deponent
    - statement_facts (text): The statements being affirmed
    - verification_place (text): Place where affidavit is verified

    Optional fields:
    - case_reference (text): Related case number if any
    - exhibit_list (text): List of exhibits attached

    **PETITION templates (3 files):**

    Required fields:
    - petitioner_name (party): Full name of petitioner
    - petitioner_address (text): Address of petitioner
    - respondent_name (party): Full name of respondent
    - respondent_address (text): Address of respondent
    - cause_of_action (text): Facts giving rise to petition
    - relief_sought (relief): Specific reliefs/prayers
    - jurisdiction_ground (text): Basis for court's jurisdiction

    Optional fields:
    - interim_relief (text): Any interim reliefs sought
    - court_fee_details (text): Court fee affixed

    **Formatting variations by court level:**

    Supreme Court:
    - formatting.font_size: 14
    - formatting.margin_left_right: "4cm"
    - All defaults from FormattingRequirements

    High Court:
    - formatting.font_size: 14
    - formatting.margin_left_right: "3.5cm"
    - formatting.margin_top_bottom: "2.5cm"

    District Court:
    - formatting.font_size: 12
    - formatting.margin_left_right: "3cm"
    - formatting.margin_top_bottom: "2cm"

    All templates:
    - version: "1.0.0"
    - created_at and updated_at: use current ISO timestamp
    - Descriptions should explain the template purpose and legal context
